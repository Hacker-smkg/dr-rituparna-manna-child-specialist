About the Doctor

Where Every Child Gets the Care They Deserve

Dr. Rituparna Manna is one of Kalyani's most trusted and loved child specialists, known for her warm, child-friendly approach and clinical excellence. She has built a reputation for making children feel comfortable and parents feel reassured at every visit.

Her practice covers the entire spectrum of childhood healthcare — from managing newborn health in the first days of life, through the critical early years of growth and development, to adolescent medicine. Dr. Manna combines academic rigour with genuine empathy, ensuring each child receives individualized, evidence-based care.

Her clinic in Kalyani is known for its child-friendly environment, minimal waiting times, and thorough consultations. Parents across Nadia trust Dr. Manna as their go-to child specialist for everything from routine checkups and vaccinations to managing complex paediatric conditions.

Our Services

Comprehensive Paediatric Care

From the very first day of life through adolescence — expert care at every stage of your child's journey.

🍼

Newborn & Neonatal Care

Complete health assessment for newborns including birth examination, jaundice management, feeding support, weight monitoring and early developmental checks.

💉

Vaccination & Immunisation

Comprehensive vaccine schedules following IAP and government guidelines for all ages. Expert counselling on vaccine safety and importance for parents.

📏

Growth & Development Monitoring

Regular assessment of height, weight, head circumference and developmental milestones. Early identification of delays and timely specialist referral.

🌡️

Fever & Paediatric Infections

Diagnosis and treatment of childhood fevers, viral and bacterial infections, typhoid, dengue, respiratory infections, UTIs, and skin infections.

🫁

Respiratory & Asthma Care

Management of asthma, wheezing, recurrent cough, bronchitis, and pneumonia in children with nebulisation guidance and long-term management plans.

🥗

Nutrition & Feeding Guidance

Breastfeeding support, weaning advice, nutrition counselling for underweight or overweight children, iron deficiency anaemia management and dietary planning.

🧠

Behavioural & Developmental Issues

Assessment of speech delays, ADHD, learning difficulties, autism spectrum concerns, sleep problems, and school-related behavioural issues with appropriate referral.

🏥

General Paediatric OPD

Routine well-child checkups, school fitness certificates, pre-travel advice, management of chronic childhood conditions, second opinions, and report review.

👦

Adolescent Health

Healthcare for teenagers covering puberty-related concerns, adolescent nutrition, acne, menstrual health guidance, mental wellness counselling and lifestyle advice.

FAQ

Parents Ask, We Answer

Common questions about paediatric consultations, vaccinations, and appointments.

At what age should I bring my newborn for the first visit?
Ideally within 3–5 days of hospital discharge. Dr. Manna will assess weight, feeding, jaundice, and overall health, and guide you on the vaccination schedule.
Does Dr. Manna follow the IAP vaccination schedule?
Yes. Dr. Manna follows the Indian Academy of Paediatrics (IAP) recommended schedule and counsels parents on optional vaccines to give their children the best protection.
My child has a fever — should I book an appointment?
For fever above 102°F, lasting more than 48 hours, or with rash/vomiting/breathing difficulty, please book urgently. Select "Urgent Consultation" in the booking form.
How often should I bring my child for routine checkups?
Under 2 years: well-child visits at 1, 2, 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18 and 24 months. After age 2, annual checkups are advisable. Dr. Manna will guide you on the ideal schedule.
Does Dr. Manna see adolescent patients (teenagers)?
Yes. Dr. Manna provides paediatric care up to 18 years, including puberty concerns, nutrition, acne, menstrual health guidance, mental wellness, and school health advice.
How will I receive my appointment confirmation?
The clinic will contact you by phone or WhatsApp within 2 hours during OPD hours (9 AM–8 PM, Monday to Saturday) to confirm your exact appointment time.
